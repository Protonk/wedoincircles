## Cluster 1 — Methodological frame

Seven memos that fix the program's cost framework, vocabulary, citation discipline, L-W audit posture, and substrate-vocabulary refusals. None carries theorem content; all govern how theorem content gets stated and admitted.

**`COST-MODEL-UNIFORMITY-BRIEF.md`** (memos/) — pairs Cook–Reckhow 1973 with Slot–van Emde Boas 1984/1988 to fix the cost-model vocabulary the program operates inside. **`COASE-FRICTION-AND-SPECIALISTS.md`** (measure/) — vocabulary memo importing Coase 1937 *The Nature of the Firm* as the typing surface for the transaction cost `δ`.
